Mint AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mint AI (“Mint”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Mint mobile application and its backend service. By using the app you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information. When you register, we store your username, email address, and a securely hashed password. We never store your password in plain text.
  • Financial data you enter. Income and expense transactions, accounts, categories, investment portfolios, investment transactions, and the holdings you track. This data is associated with your account so it can be synced and backed up.
  • AI assistant content. The messages you send to the in-app assistant, and any images you choose to attach, are transmitted to our service to generate a response.
  • Technical data. Basic request metadata (such as IP address and timestamps) is processed by our servers to operate the service securely and to apply rate limiting.

We do not access your photo library unless you explicitly choose to attach an image to an assistant conversation. We do not collect location data, contacts, or advertising identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core features: recording your finances, tracking portfolios, and syncing/backing up your data.
  • To generate responses from the AI assistant based on the messages and images you submit.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To maintain, debug, and protect the reliability and security of the service.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

To deliver certain features, limited data is shared with the following processors:

  • AI provider (Google Gemini). Your assistant messages and any attached images are processed by Google’s generative AI service to produce a response.
  • Web search (Tavily). When the assistant needs current information, a search query derived from your request may be sent to a search provider.
  • Market data providers (e.g. CoinGecko, Coinbase, CoinMarketCap, metals.dev, and exchange-rate services). These receive only asset symbols needed to fetch prices — not your personal information.

Each provider processes data under its own privacy policy.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account exists. You can permanently delete your account at any time from Settings → Account → Delete account in the app. Deleting your account permanently removes your user record and all associated server-side data — including your accounts, categories, transactions, portfolios, investment transactions, snapshots, and backups. This action cannot be undone.

5. How We Protect Your Data

  • All network traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS).
  • Passwords are stored using strong one-way hashing (Argon2).
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

Mint is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. You can delete all of your data directly in the app as described above, or contact us for assistance.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
